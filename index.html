    OUR PHILOSOPHY

    The Glen Back Care & Sports Therapy

    Exceptional care with effective hands-on treatment

    • Philosophy
    • Treatment
    • Founding

    The Glen Back Care & Sports Therapy is the go-to clinic in the Waverley area for exceptional care and hands-on treatment aimed at preventing and treating various musculoskeletal conditions, including spinal pain, sports injuries, and chronic pain.

    We create personalized treatment plans that aim to relieve pain quickly and address the root cause for long-term results. Our goal is to work with you to get you back to doing the things you love and feeling your best.

    Whether you're dealing with recurring pain, post-traumatic injuries like whiplash, or looking to maintain your mobility and independence, we're here to help.

    Our approach focuses on providing long-term solutions that promote overall health and well-being, rather than just temporary pain relief.

    We work with competitive athletes to help them perform at their peak physical capacity, and with everyone else to help them feel and function their best.

    Since our founding in 1993, The Glen Back Care & Sports Therapy has been the clinic of choice in the Waverley area.

    We provide exceptional care, with effective hands-on treatment in the privacy of your own treatment room.

    We have helped literally thousands of people to feel better and to function at their best, and we can help you too!
